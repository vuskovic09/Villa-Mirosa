About Us

Villa Mirosa is located on one of the most green and beautiful islands in Croatia in the small place Saplunara- the island of Mljet. It is close to the sea, surrounded by pine woods which makes it a real small paradise for the lovers of untouched nature and crystal clear seas. Within the Villa perimiter there is a private stony beach and a beautiful pool with sea water from which there is an amazing view on the sea and Elafit islands. Only 5 minutes of relaxed stroll away are the sandy beaches – Small and Big Saplunara. On the villa’s ground floor there is a restaurant where we offer various fish and meat dishes of the traditional Dalmatian cuisine prepared on the grill or “under the lid”. Pansion guests have a three course meal for dinner and they can choose between meat, fish and vegetarian menu. We are especially proud of the quality of the food products purchased exclusively on eco farmers markets and, we ourselves, produce olive oil, wines and spirits.

Lokacija i informacije

Saplunara village and cove are located in the south-eastern part of the southern coast of the island of Mljet. The cove is enclosed by a thick forest of pine. It consists of Velika and Mala Saplunara. The sea in the large shallows reaches a temperature of over 28 °C during the summer. The regional road via Korita connects it with other towns and villages on the island and with the National Park Mljet. A village with crystal clear seawater, beautiful sand beaches, a restaurant and rec-reation opportunities – especially attractive for nature enthusiasts who prefer a peaceful holiday. The picturesque cove of Blaca (beach, pine forest) is very close to Saplunara. By the road to the village Korita lies an area of stone pine forests of rare beauty, called Pinjevci. The legend has it that in AD 61 St. Paul the Apostle used to stay there after a shipwreck.
